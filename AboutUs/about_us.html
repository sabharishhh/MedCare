About Us

60 Years of Excellence in Healing : Together, we embark on a new era of compassionate healthcare

Certain legacies transcend time, leaving an indelible mark on the hearts of those who experience them. Med Care Hospital is one such legacy—a beacon of medical excellence and compassionate care. Since its humble beginnings, Med Care has been a trusted name in healthcare, offering generations of patients the sacred gift of healing, infused with empathy and dedication. The story of Med Care is one of growth, service, and an unwavering commitment to the well-being of the community.

Founded in 1960, Med Care Hospital began as a small clinic with just 10 beds and one dedicated doctor. It was established with a vision by Dr. John Mathew, a pioneer in medical service, who aimed to create a healthcare institution that provided quality medical care with a human touch. What started as a modest venture has now blossomed into a world-class, multi-specialty hospital known for its excellence in patient care.

Today, Med Care stands tall as a comprehensive healthcare hub, providing advanced medical services under one roof. With a capacity of over 500 beds, the hospital houses 40+ specialized departments staffed by more than 150 expert doctors and supported by a dedicated team of over 1200 healthcare professionals. We pride ourselves on having the latest medical technologies, including state-of-the-art MRI and CT scanners, fully equipped operating theaters, intensive care units, and a comprehensive diagnostic center.

Med Care Hospital's dedication to cutting-edge medical technology is reflected in its infrastructure, which includes advanced imaging facilities like the Philips 3T MRI Scanner, GE Revolution CT Scanner, Siemens Digital Cath Lab, and Philips 4D Ultrasound. In addition, our hospital offers 24/7 pharmacy, laboratory, radiology, and emergency services, ensuring that patients receive seamless and efficient care around the clock.

At Med Care, we don't just treat diseases; we care for people. Our ethos blends clinical excellence with a deep sense of empathy, ensuring that every patient who walks through our doors feels supported, comforted, and valued. For over six decades, we have been a trusted partner in health, offering a blend of quality, compassion, and innovation that touches lives and fosters healing.

Med Care Hospital—Where care meets compassion, and every patient experience is a step towards holistic well-being.
